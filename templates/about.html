About EEG Predictor

With the advances in machine learning, this app is able to perform the pre-diagnosis step for you. It can identify your EEG file (.edf) as Normal or Abnormal. If your EEG file is predicted as Abnormal, the problem might be serious. You must consult your respective neurologist immediately.
